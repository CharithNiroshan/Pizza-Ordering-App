Who We Are

Bruno's Pizzaeria was bought to Australia in the early 80's, by an inspired young man named Bruno. Having scored the perfect location, withtin the heart of Hawthorn, Bruno set out to bring his italian heritage to everyone's bellies.

The reason you're probably looking at our website right now is becuase you're looking for dinner. We can guarantee you'll have the biggest smile and happiest belly after taking your first bite of our delicious pizzas.

If you're loooking to dine-in, we can provide proffessional restaurant quality service. Or if you're looking to binge your favourite TV show on the couch, we can deliver right to your door.
